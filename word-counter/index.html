Frequently Asked Questions

How is reading time calculated?

Reading time is based on an average reading speed of 200 words per minute for adults. Speaking time uses 130 WPM.

Does this tool store my text?

No. All analysis is done locally in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server.

What counts as a word?

Any sequence of characters separated by spaces. Punctuation attached to words is included with the word (e.g., "hello," counts as one word).
